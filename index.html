Suzzallo Highlights

History

Opened in 1926 and named for UW president Henry Suzzallo.

Architecture

Collegiate Gothic by Bebb & Gould—stained glass, stone tracery, tall lancet windows.

Must-See

  • Graduate Reading Room
  • Grand façade on Red Square
  • Ornate woodwork & carvings
